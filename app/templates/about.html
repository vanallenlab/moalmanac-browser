About

The Molecular Oncology Almanac (MOAlmanac) is a clinical and biological interpretation algorithm and paired knowledge base for the precision oncology. This webpage and version of the database is currently in development. You can view our stable, production version of the algorithm and database at: https://moalmanac.org. This major release aims to align with representation standards developed by the Global Alliance for Genomics & Health Genomic Knowledge Standards Work Stream. You can read about our progress, here.

This website is specifically for browsing and accessing the underlying knowledge base, and there are several other resources within the Molecular Oncology Almanac ecosystem.

  • Molecular Oncology Almanac API, a service to programmatically interface with our knowledge base [GitHub].
  • Molecular Oncology Almanac Browser, a website to browse our underlying knowledge base [GitHub].
  • Molecular Oncology Almanac Database, the content and release notes of our underlying knowledge base [GitHub].
The original version of this website can be found at: https://moalmanac.org.

Additionally, we support region-specific instances of knowledge base. They currently include:

  • The Republic of Ireland. https://ie.moalmanac.org.
